Your privacy matters to us. At Vibe n Walls, we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or place an order.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our website.

Information you provide:

  • Name, phone number, and email address when you contact us or place an order
  • Delivery address and billing details for order fulfillment
  • Wall photos or images you share with us for custom design requests
  • Messages and communications via WhatsApp, email, or our website

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited, time spent, and clicks on our website
  • Referral source (how you found us)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and personalize our services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and custom wallpaper requests
  • Send order confirmations, design previews, and delivery updates
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • Send promotional offers and product updates (you may opt out at any time)
  • Analyze website usage to improve our products and user experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: Your name and address are shared with our logistics and courier partners solely to deliver your order.
  • Payment Processors: Payment information is handled by trusted, encrypted payment gateways. We do not store your full card details.
  • Installation Teams: Your contact details may be shared with our certified installation partners if you opt for expert installation.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
Note: All third parties we work with are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and use it only for the stated purpose.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS) for all data transmissions
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our data collection and storage practices
  • Customer photos and wall images are stored securely and deleted after order completion unless you request otherwise

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your account credentials confidential.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site usage.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., cart, session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show you relevant ads on other platforms (e.g., Meta Pixel)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time via the link in our emails
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Vibenwalls@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, social media platforms, and payment gateways. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
